What’s New This Month: February 2016

By Jessica Desjardins
New Products - 7 days ago

What’s New This Month: February 2016

Want the scoop on the latest and greatest products to hit the shelves? Here are 12 brand new products to put on your radar this month, from makeup to skincare to fragrance and more; all available now. You’ve been warned—some serious enabling is about to happen!

Let me know which products you’re putting on your wishlist and if you have any others to add. Keep on scrolling to read about ’em all!

Click here to discover each product on Beautezine!
  • 1 of 12

  • Benefit Dew the Hoola Liquid Bronzer, $28

    Benefit’s cult classic Hoola bronzer is now available in a soft matte, liquid version! The lightweight gel smooths onto bare skin with ease for an all-over faux glow, or can be used on top of foundation for a more precise bronzing effect. Available at Sephora.

  • 2 of 12

  • Bite Beauty Amuse Bouche Lipstick, $26

    Get ready for one of Bite’s biggest launches to date! The new Amuse Bouche lipstick collection features a whopping 34 new shades from soft nudes to bold colors to vampy plums, each formulated to deliver extreme hydration, saturated color and creamy wear. Plus—they have a delicious citrus flavor made from fresh pressed fruit—yum! Available this month at Sephora.

  • 3 of 12

  • Chloe Love Story Eau de Toilette, $90 CA

    A lighter version of Chloe’s original Love Story has landed just in time for Valentine’s Day! This fresh, floral scent is the perfect choice for a romantic, feminine fragrance for Spring. Available currently in Canada at Sephora..

  • 4 of 12

  • Fresh Seaberry Skin Nutrution Cleansing Oil, $42

    A new addition to Fresh’s skin-loving Seaberry line has arrived! This cleansing oil features a blend of nourishing oils and protective extracts to help break down stubborn makeup (even waterproof mascara!), impurities and oil—all while maintaining your skin’s natural moisture barrier. Available at Sephora.

  • 5 of 12

  • Sephora Color Switch By Vera Mona Brush Cleaner, $18

    This genius tool is a must-have for any beauty addict! The innovative sponge material helps to knock off pigment and powder from your makeup brushes so you can use the same brush with a new color. It’s washable and re-usable too! Available at Sephora.

  • 6 of 12

  • Estée Lauder Double Wear Waterproof All Day Extreme Wear Concealer, $25

    As a bonafide concealer junkie (seriously, these dark circles need some serious help), I’m so excited to try Estée Lauder’s new, ultimate concealer. The formula promises 24-hour wear without creasing, streaking or caking. Plus, it’s waterproof, sweatproof and transfer-resistant… so those dark circles will be covered without worry! Available this month at Sephora.

  • 7 of 12

  • Philosophy Amazing Grace 20th Birthday Room Spray, $20

    It’s hard to believe Amazing Grace is turning 20 years old this year! The iconic fragrance is one of my all-time favorites, and they are celebrating with an array of special-edition products like this Amazing Grace Room Spray. If you’re like me, you want to smell this feminine, clean scent all day long and this is the perfect product to make that happen! Available at Sephora.

  • 8 of 12

  • Bliss Climate Control, $129

    Now here’s a unique tool I’ve never seen anything like before! This facial wand is designed to use with your favorite skincare products. The tool heats up to help your creams and serums to absorb better and penetrate deeper as well as cool down to help shrink pores, reduce redness and puffiness. How handy! Available at Bliss.

  • 9 of 12

  • Atelier Cologne Collection Orient, $250

    This luxurious fragrance collection features five scents, each inspired by the precious raw materials of Asia and the Middle East. Encased in white glass bottles, it’s Atelier Cologne’s most luxe collection to date. The scents include Mimosa Indigo, Encens Jinhae, Philtre Ceylan, Poive Electrique and Tobacco Nuit. Available at at Atelier Cologne.

  • 10 of 12

  • Chanel Le Blanc Brightening Pearl Soap, $60

    Chanel WOULD be the only brand out there that would tempt me to shell out $60 for a bar of soap. Well, a pearl of soap (so much more luxurious, right)? All kidding aside, this makeup remover soap is actually amazing. The scent is to die for (and I don’t even LIKE fragrance in skincare) and it lathers so beautifully to really get every last stitch of makeup off. And of course, it’s a pearl. Available at Nordstrom.

  • 11 of 12

  • Giorgio Armani Maestro Glow Foundation, $64

    As a sequel to Giorgio Armani’s revolutionary Maestro foundation, this luminous bi-phase makeup is sure to make an even stronger impression. This foundation leaves skin with a luxurious, silky sheen (I’ve described it as panty hose for your face—you’ll understand when you try). It’s so lightweight, you’ll forget you’re wearing anything at all—but your skin will look super healthy, glowing and hydrated. Available this month at Sephora.

  • 12 of 12

  • T3 Featherweight Compact Folding Dryer, $150

    I just finished talking about my favorite hair dryers, and of course, T3 launches this travel-friendly version of one of them! This compact, folding version of their best-selling Featherweight Hair Dryer comes in both white and black, and features all the power of it’s full-size big sister—yet folds in half for small-space storage or travel. Available at Sephora.
